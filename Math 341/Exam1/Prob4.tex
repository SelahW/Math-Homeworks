\section{Problem 4}
Prove that $12\notdivby5$.
\begin{proof}
Assume 12 \emph{is} divisible by 5. Then 12=5c, for some \textbf{integer} c. This means that $c=\frac{12}{5}$, which is not an integer, thus we have a contradiction, so $12\notdivby5$.
\end{proof}
