Let $X$ be a space and $P$ a path component of $X$. Prove that $P$ is a subset of some component of $X$.\begin{proof}Let $C$ be a component of $X$, $x$ a point of $C$ and $P$ be the path component of $X$ which contains $x$.\skip Then, since $P$ is by definition path connected, it must also be connected. Since $P$ is connected, $P\subset C$.\end{proof}\end{document}
